    
Introduction 
    The objective of this project is to create a device that can listen and interpret user’s 
speach to execute preprogrammed scripts while maintaining transparency in data usage and 
maximising secutity. In the following report, we will evaluate similar products and our 
criteria for evaluating our prototype. 

1. Analysis of needs 

    The following list presents some weaknesses of current smart speakers. The weaknesses 
should guide our team in how we decide to develop our prototype. 

    - All requests get sent to a server (not private) 

    - Network dependent (only works if you have internet) 

    - Locked into an ecosystem 

    - Services that may be beloved are removed while others that are unappreciated are 
    pushed 

    - Devices rely on matching words rather than intent (not a real conversation) 

    - Bad contextual awareness 

2. Objectives 

	    The primary objective of this project is to develop a smart speaker that can operate 
    using a local ai to perform web searches and execute preprogrammed scripts. 

	- For it to perform to our standards, we need it to: 

    - Recognize user’s speech and interact in a conversational manner (less need for precise wording) 

    - Be able to run an AI model to recognize words and interpret to execute commands locally 

    - Deliver quick and reasonable responses  

    - Be intuitive to use by young and old 

    - Detect the wake command despite noise and from a distance 

3. Restrictions 

This section will explain self-imposed restrictions so that the device and software are 
reasonably priced and competitive with other devices available on the market. 

    - The the baseline Apple smart speaker and Google smart speaker each cost 100$ while 
    the Amazon smart speaker costs 50$. Considering these prices, our product should 
    definitely not cost more than 100$ and we should prefer cheaper options. 

    - The device should be able to operate offline 

    - It should be able to connect to other devices using the local wifi network, while 
    not using the internet.
